{% trans "About the CRVA Portal" %}

{% blocktrans %} The Climate Risk and Vulnerability Assessment (CRVA) Portal is an interactive web-based platform designed to support climate-resilient agriculture in Mozambique. It delivers accessible tools and data for informed decision-making at local, regional, and national levels. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "The portal integrates the following key services:" %}

  • {% trans "Agro-Weather Advisory:" %} {% trans "Provides timely weather forecasts and alerts to assist farmers with scheduling critical activities such as planting, irrigation, and harvesting." %}
  • {% trans "Crop Advisory:" %} {% trans "Offers location-specific agronomic guidance on best crop practices, pest management, and nutrient application." %}
  • {% trans "Market Information:" %} {% trans "Supplies data on crop prices, market demand, and logistics to improve farmersâ€™ access to markets and maximize income." %}
  • {% trans "Climate Risk & Vulnerability Assessment:" %} {% trans "Identifies regions most at risk from climate shocks like droughts or floods, enabling targeted adaptation and planning." %}
  • {% trans "Adaptation Strategies:" %} {% trans "Suggests tailored resilience measures, including climate-smart agriculture practices, soil management, and water conservation." %}

{% blocktrans %} This portal was developed by the Alliance of Bioversity International and CIAT, in collaboration with local partners, to enhance food security and resilience to climate change in Mozambique. {% endblocktrans %}
